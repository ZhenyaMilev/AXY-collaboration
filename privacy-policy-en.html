AXY Marketing Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AXY Marketing ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our website (the "Site") and our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Contact Information: your name, phone number, email address, and Telegram username when you fill out an audit or consultation request form.
  • Business Information: information about your store, brand, and business goals that you provide during our communication.

We may also collect information automatically when you visit our Site:

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type and version, device information, operating system.
  • Usage Data: pages you visited, time spent on the site, and other activities on the Site.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services, including conducting audits and consultations.
  • To communicate with you, respond to your inquiries, and send commercial offers.
  • To analyze usage of our Site to improve its functionality and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your activity on our Site. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can configure your browser to refuse all cookies or to notify you when they are being sent.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of data transmission over the Internet or electronic storage method is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information that we store. You may also object to the processing of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at account@axyglobal.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We recommend periodically reviewing this page for changes.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us:

  • By email: account@axyglobal.com
